Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Onset is an interactive DJ learning platform that uses your browser's audio and MIDI capabilities to provide hands-on training. This Privacy Policy explains what information Onset collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Onset, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

CategoryWhat we collectWhy
Account information Email address, username, password hash (via Supabase Auth) Authentication and account management
Learning progress Completed lessons, scores, skill levels, practice session history Tracking your progress and personalizing the curriculum
Browser/device data Browser type, OS, screen resolution, audio/MIDI device capabilities Compatibility and performance optimization
Usage data Pages visited, features used, session duration, error events Improving the platform and diagnosing issues
Audio processing: Audio processed through the Web Audio API is handled entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not record, store, or analyze your microphone input or audio output.

Local Storage and Browser Data

Onset uses browser localStorage to persist the following data locally on your device:

  • Current lesson and module progress (before sync to account)
  • Audio and MIDI configuration preferences
  • UI settings and layout preferences
  • Session tokens provided by Supabase Auth

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted except as described in this policy. You can clear it at any time via your browser's storage settings.

Web Audio and Web MIDI APIs

Onset uses the Web Audio API and Web MIDI API to provide interactive DJ training. These APIs are browser-native and operate entirely client-side. Your browser will prompt you for permission before granting access to audio output or MIDI devices.

We do not intercept, record, or transmit audio signals or MIDI data to our servers. All audio and MIDI processing occurs locally in your browser in real time.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Provides authentication and database services. Stores your account and progress data. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Vercel: Hosts and serves the application. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your account and progress data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information at any time via settings
  • Portability: Request an export of your learning history

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@onset.app.

Data Retention

We retain your account and progress data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days and from backups within 90 days.

Children's Privacy

Onset is intended for users 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
