# Onset — Product Brief

## One-liner
Interactive DJ lessons in the browser with real MIDI controller support.

## Problem
Learning to DJ is broken. YouTube tutorials are passive — you watch someone else mix, then fumble around on your controller with no feedback. DJ schools cost $50-150/hour. Software like Serato and Rekordbox are built for performing DJs, not learners. There's no "Duolingo for DJing" — structured, interactive, with real-time feedback on your actual moves.

## Solution
Onset is a web app that teaches DJing through interactive lessons. You plug in your MIDI controller (or use your phone as a controller via Onset Connect), load real audio files, and follow guided exercises. The app watches your inputs in real-time and scores you on accuracy, timing, and phrase alignment — like Guitar Hero for DJ skills.

Key features:
- Watch-Imagine-Do lesson structure (see it, visualize it, do it)
- Real-time scoring with accuracy, timing, and phrase alignment
- Spaced repetition for technique retention
- Adaptive difficulty based on rolling accuracy
- XP system with levels and streaks
- BPM detection and beat grid alignment
- Works with Hercules DJControl Inpulse 200 MK2 (expandable to other controllers)

## Target User
- **Primary**: 16-30 year olds who want to learn to DJ but haven't started or just bought their first controller
- **Secondary**: Bedroom DJs who are self-taught and want structured practice
- **Tertiary**: Music producers who want to add live performance skills

## Market Size
- **Classification**: broad-consumer
- DJ controller market: ~$500M globally, growing 8% YoY
- Online music education: ~$4B globally
- Addressable: ~2M people actively searching "how to DJ" or buying entry-level controllers per year
- There are 30K+ monthly searches for "learn to DJ" and "DJ tutorial" on Google

## Competitors
| Name | What | Gap |
|------|------|-----|
| YouTube tutorials | Passive video | No interactivity, no feedback, no structure |
| Crossfader (crossfader.co.uk) | Online DJ school | $30/month, video-based, no real-time feedback |
| DJ TechTools | Articles/videos | Content, not software |
| Melodics | Interactive drum/keys practice | No DJ focus, no MIDI controller mapping |
| Pioneer DJ tutorial mode | Built into hardware | Only works on Pioneer gear ($800+) |

## Differentiator
Only product that gives real-time, scored feedback on actual DJ controller inputs in the browser. No download, no expensive hardware requirement (phone controller coming), structured curriculum with adaptive difficulty.

## Business Model
- **Free tier**: Core lessons, practice mode, phone controller
- **Premium** (future): Advanced lessons, custom tracks, detailed analytics, multiple controller support
- **Hardware affiliate**: Partner with controller manufacturers (Hercules, Pioneer) for referral revenue
