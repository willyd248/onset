Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a DJ controller?

Not yet. You can use mouse and keyboard for basic lessons. But a controller like the Hercules Inpulse 200 MK2 (~$100) makes it 10x better. We're also building a phone-as-controller feature.

What music can I practice with?

Any audio files you own — MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC. Drag them onto a deck and go. Onset auto-detects BPM and generates a beat grid.

Is it free?

Core features are free forever. No credit card, no trial period. We'll offer premium lessons and analytics later, but the fundamentals stay free.

What browsers work?

Chrome, Edge, and Firefox on desktop (1200px+ screen). Web Audio and Web MIDI APIs are required. Safari has limited MIDI support.
