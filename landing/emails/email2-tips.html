Day 3 — Lesson Tips

Your first mix is closer than you think.

Whether you've already fired up Lesson 1 or are still getting set up — here's what separates a smooth first mix from a frustrating one.

3 things beginners get wrong

Mistake 1

Rushing the EQ before the beats are matched

Get the tempos locked first. Fading in a track that's even slightly off-beat sounds worse than a hard cut. BPM sync first, EQ blend second.

Mistake 2

Using headphones at max volume

Ear fatigue sets in fast. Keep cue mix at 60–70% — you only need to hear the incoming track enough to match the kick drum, nothing more.

Mistake 3

Skipping the Imagine phase

Onset's Watch → Imagine → Do flow exists for a reason. The mental rehearsal step isn't filler — it primes your hands for what's coming. Don't skip it to get to the knobs faster.
